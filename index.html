Circle Hero
Instructions

You are playing as Circle who is trying to rescue weaker circles from Dr. Evil Triangle. These circles will be around the triangle for you to free. The circles that you take from evil triangle will be tallied on the top leftcorner of the screen. Try to beat your highscore and share this game with your friends and see who has the highest score among you all. What are you waiting for?!! Save them circles!!!!
